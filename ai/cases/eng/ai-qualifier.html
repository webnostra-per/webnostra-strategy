The Solution

Facebook Ads ‚Üí Messenger AI qualifier that answers, qualifies, and routes users to the right next step

1

Messenger AI Qualifier

Single channel, one clear flow. The bot starts immediately after the user clicks the Facebook ad and starts messaging. Conversation stays short and structured: service area check (postcode/city), customer type (home/business), need (new connection/upgrade/switching), key parameters (speed, contract, timing), and best next step: schedule a call or visit an office. Edge cases (complaints, urgent outages, aggressive tone) are immediately routed to a human.

2

Instant answers + "decision-ready" messaging

The bot responds in seconds with: clear plan recommendations (2‚Äì3 options, no overload), simple pricing framing (starting from / typical range), what's needed to proceed (address, preferred installation window), and a single CTA at each step (book a call / get directions to office).

3

Automated handoff to call or office

When the user is ready: the bot proposes available call windows (or asks for preferred time). If the user prefers in-person, it routes them to the nearest office and collects a preferred visit time and date. The sales team receives a clean summary: intent, address, plan interest, urgency, and preferred contact method.

4

Quality control + sales funnel visibility

Full Messenger funnel tracking: Enquiry ‚Üí Qualification ‚Üí Appointment Intent ‚Üí Scheduled Call / Office Visit ‚Üí Attended ‚Üí Closed/Won. This made it obvious where drop-offs happened and which replies needed refinement.
